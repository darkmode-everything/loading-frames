Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Yes, completely free. If you find it useful, you can support the project on Ko-fi — but it's never required.
Loading Frames is not distributed through the Mac App Store. To open it, right-click the app and choose Open, then click Open again in the dialog. You only need to do this once.
Loading Frames is currently built for Apple Silicon (M1 and later). Intel Mac support may be added in a future release.
Age-restricted or private videos require YouTube authentication. On first launch, the app guides you through a one-time setup to connect your browser cookies. You can also do this anytime in Settings → YouTube Authentication.
Normal quality encodes to H.264 MP4 — compatible with QuickTime and every Apple device. Best quality downloads the original streams as MKV with zero re-encoding, giving you the exact quality YouTube serves. MKV files can be converted to MP4 with one click inside the app.
Loading Frames is a tool. Downloading content for personal offline use is generally tolerated in many jurisdictions, but redistributing copyrighted content is not. Always respect the rights of content creators.
By default, files are saved to a "Loading Frames Downloads" folder inside your Downloads directory. You can change this anytime in Settings → Download Location.
